  • WHAT IS PRODIGY?
  • Prodigy is an internal tool designed for hotels, third-party hotel managers, and Hilton team members to manage the products and services offered to guests. It was developed to consolidate and modernize the way these offerings are housed, reducing the need for multiple applications and streamlining hotel operations through a single, updated platform.
